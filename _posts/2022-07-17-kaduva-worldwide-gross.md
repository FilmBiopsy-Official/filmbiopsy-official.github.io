**Kaduva** directed by Shaji Kailas starring Prithviraj and Vivek Oberoi has crossed 38 Crores Gross from Worldwide Theatricals.

Movie made on Budget of 18 Crores has completed 12,000 shows from Kerala BoxOffice.

## Kaduva Worldwide Gross
- Kerala - 20.2 Cr
- GCC - 15.8 Cr
- Rest Of India - 1.3 Cr
- Rest Of World - 0.93 Cr
- **Grand Total - 38.23 Crores**
